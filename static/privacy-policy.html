Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Summary: Fanout Publisher is designed with privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service. Your video content is processed temporarily and not stored permanently on our servers. Your API keys and tokens are stored securely and never shared with third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fanout Publisher ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our video publishing service.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Fanout Publisher, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Service:

Data Type Purpose Storage Duration
API Keys & Tokens To authenticate with social media platforms Until you revoke access or delete them
Video Files To upload to selected platforms Temporary (deleted after processing)
Video Metadata Title, description, tags for publishing Stored in database for tracking
Publishing History Track upload status and results Stored in database indefinitely

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service (features used, upload frequency)
  • Error Logs: Technical data when errors occur to help us improve the Service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: Process and upload your videos to selected social media platforms
  • Maintain and Improve: Monitor usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and potential security threats
  • Communication: Send you technical notices and support messages (if applicable)
  • Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database and Redis cache. Video files are stored temporarily on our server filesystem during the upload process and are automatically deleted after successful or failed publishing.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data like API keys and tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Access Control: Limited access to servers and databases
  • Secure Transmission: HTTPS for all data transmission (when properly configured)
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and dependency updates

Important: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your API keys and access tokens confidential.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Platforms

When you use Fanout Publisher to upload videos, your content is transmitted to the social media platforms you select (TikTok, VK, YouTube). Each platform has its own privacy policy:

  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • VK: https://vk.com/privacy
  • YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 We Do NOT Share Your Data With

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers or analytics services
  • Any third party for commercial purposes

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

6. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for varying periods:

  • Video Files: Deleted immediately after upload completion (successful or failed)
  • API Keys/Tokens: Stored until you revoke access or delete your configuration
  • Publishing History: Retained indefinitely for your records (you can delete individual records)
  • Log Data: Retained for 30-90 days for troubleshooting purposes

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Export: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Revocation: Revoke API access tokens at any time through the respective platforms

8. Self-Hosted Deployment

Fanout Publisher is an open-source project that you can self-host. If you deploy your own instance:

  • You are the data controller and responsible for compliance with privacy regulations
  • This Privacy Policy serves as a template; you should adapt it to your specific deployment
  • You must ensure proper security measures for your deployment
  • You are responsible for data backups and disaster recovery

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the Service from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred across international borders. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Service uses minimal cookies for session management and functionality. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics services like Google Analytics.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
